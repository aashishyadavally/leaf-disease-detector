Behind the Scenes

Labeling Images

We found an image dataset on Kaggle.com and then labeled those images in Labelbox. We had 5 plant types and had a healthy and diseased category for each, making 10 total categories.

Training the Model

Training the AI Model took around 2 days to train. Using back propagation, the model was able to teach itself how to adjust weights in the neural network to be able to improve its performance. Below is a sample of the output when the AI makes predictions.

Testing our model
